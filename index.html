What's In It

No proprietary blends. No "& other ingredients." Every active on the label is at the dose used in its human clinical study. The fine print is the whole print.

astaxanthin
keystone · 4 mg / day
One of the most-studied protectors of mitochondrial membranes — the place inside every cell where energy is produced. Sourced as AstaZine® from Algae Health Sciences — a registered ingredient brand, the first astaxanthin to carry organic certification, grown in closed-glass photobioreactors. 4mg in glow, surge, and guard. hyaluronic acid
substrate · 120 mg / day · Hyabest®
Sourced as Hyabest® from Kewpie Corporation (Japan) — a registered ingredient brand, low-MW, bio-fermentation derived. The raw material your skin cells use to hold onto water. The exact branded ingredient and dose shown in placebo-controlled trials to improve skin hydration at 12 weeks.
phytoceramides
substrate · 350 mg / day · Ceratiq®
Sourced as Ceratiq® from PLT Health Solutions (France) — a registered ingredient brand. Non-GMO French wheat, gluten-free tested per batch. The raw material your skin uses to rebuild its barrier lipids. Clinically studied at this dose to support skin hydration.
vitamins c, e, zn, se
cofactors · 100% DV each
Vitamin E protects mitochondrial lipids. Vitamin C supports enzyme reactions around ATP. Zinc and selenium defend the energy machinery. Non-negotiable architecture.

No added sugar. The sweetness comes from xylitol (the chew's structural base). Fruit-forward flavor with no crash.

Gluten-free, yes. Astaxanthin is sourced as AstaZine® (organic-certified microalgae). Phytoceramides are sourced as Ceratiq® — French non-GMO wheat, tested per batch for gluten-free conformity. The gelling system is under formulation review — vegan status will be confirmed on label at launch.

On your time. Every day, whenever fits your rhythm. We don't do prescriptive timing — that's how routines die.
